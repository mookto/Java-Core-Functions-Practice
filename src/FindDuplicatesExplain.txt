## ‚úÖ Step 2: Loop through characters

    map.put(c, map.getOrDefault(c, 0) + 1);

* `str.toCharArray()` ‚Üí string ‡¶ï‡ßá character array-‡¶è convert ‡¶ï‡¶∞‡ßá
* ‡¶™‡ßç‡¶∞‡¶§‡¶ø‡¶ü‡¶ø character `c`-‡¶è‡¶∞ ‡¶ú‡¶®‡ßç‡¶Ø:
